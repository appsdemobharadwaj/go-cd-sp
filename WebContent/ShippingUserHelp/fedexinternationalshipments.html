Fedex International Shipments

If a user selects the ShipConsole Main page, it accepts the Order Number for imported orders as the input and populates the information . If the destination address of the shipment is International (i.e. other than ship from country) we can see an International shipment link on the right side of the Shipment page as displayed in the Figure 8.1.

Figure 8.1

For international shipments click on the International Shipment link and enter all the necessary information about the shipments in the popup window.

Select Fedex as shipmethod , and click on Internationalshipment link as shown in the Figure 8.1 .

Figure 8.2 is the international shipment popup for Fedex.
